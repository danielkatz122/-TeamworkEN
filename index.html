Teamwork

Teamwork or joint work is defined as the joint activity of two or more individuals (people, animals, robots, etc.), working for a similar goal while cooperating between them. Teamwork is mainly characterized by groups.

Advantages-

  • Higher utilization of the labor force
  • Each team member can contribute from his abilities in a different area
  • Working together is more pleasant

Disadvantages-

  • Difficulty coordinating times
  • Multiple opinions
